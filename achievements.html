Achievements

Enactus National Competition 2020

The members of this organization have time and again proved their dedication and success by bringing laurels for the college. Enactus Thapar showed their hard work and dedication at the national level by reaching the semi-final stage of Enactus Nationals this year, beating 95+ Enactuses from around the nation, making them one of the top 24 in the country. They also proved their community outreach at the National level by finishing among the top six most viewed as well as most voted social media channels at Enactus National cup.

Winning at IIT Roorkee’s E-Summit

Extremely motivated Enactus members Akshat Bhatia , Aryaman, Himanshu Panda, Vishu Chauhan and Parth Mishra went to the E-Summit held at IIT Roorkee on 1st and 2nd February 2020. Their team took part in the Ideathon of this nation-wide event and secured First Overall Position, bagging 2.5 Lakhs in Prizes.

About the Competition:

This competition consists of 3 rounds starting with giving a brief description of your product/service in about 250 words. A total of 650 teams applied out of which only 45 were selected on the basis of the description they provided. These teams moved on to the second round where a pitch of 3 minutes had to be delivered in front of a panel of 5 judges. After being judged on some specified guidelines, top 10 teams were selected and allowed for round three. Moving on to the last round, here a detailed pitch of 6 minutes had to be delivered that explained both the business and the technical aspect of the product being presented. Followed by the pitch was the round of rigorous questioning by the panel of big investors. This was the last round where only one team would be selected and crowned as the winner due to its excellent idea and strongest business aspect.
