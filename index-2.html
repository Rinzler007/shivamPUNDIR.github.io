W E A R E E N A C T U S

Thapar

Enactus is an international organization that connects student, academic and business leaders through entrepreneurial-based projects that empower people to transform opportunities into real, sustainable progress for themselves and their communities. Here at Enactus Thapar, we are a team of passionate and creative individuals that are shaping the future in our own way. Each of us is a collaborator at heart, and we never plan to stop!

Why Join Us

We believe investing in students who take Entrepreneurial Action for others Creates a better world for us all.

To Create Impact and Social Change

By joining Enactus Thapar you will be part of a team that is dedicated to making the world a better place. We are committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of lives.

Real World Experience

The best way to prepare for the real world is by facing it. Enactus Thapar provides you with exactly the opportunity you need. Enactus is supported by some of the best companies in the world, who recognize the value of Enactus experience and compete aggressively for the opportunity to recruit Enactus students.

Represent Us Globally

Enactus Thapar isn't a club or society, we are family. Joining Enactus Thapar makes you part of the family & you'll represent Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology,Patiala within the Enactus network and the global community.
