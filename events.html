Enactus Day 1.0

March 24-25, 2018

Enactus Day 1.0 was a huge success and attracted a lot of students to explore this field and society.On the first day, first edition of our flagship event, Social Budgeting Convention. 60 students were divided into 30 teams. Each team was given a real existing village along with some basic statistics and problems going on in the village. Colonel Gurmeet Sethi witnessed the event as the chief guest. The basic idea was to widen the horizons and change the perspective on the entrepreneurship landscape in India. An inhouse competition to bring out the best of something Enactus is truly adept at: social entrepreneurship.

India@75

January 24, 2018

Enactus Thapar organized an Educational Drive in the School Of Deaf and Blind, Patiala. Various activities for the students of age group 8-15 years including Diya Painting Competition for Juniors, Mechanic Model Building workshop for middle wing, Introduction to HTML Programming and WebPage Development for Senior batches as well as a seminar on Self- Defence by an Expert to all the students were conducted.

One of the newest figures to emerge on the world stage in recent years is the social entrepreneur. This is usually someone who burns with desire to make a positive social impact on the world, but believes that the best way of doing it is, as the saying goes, not by giving poor people a fish and feeding them for a day, but by teaching them to fish, in hopes of feeding them for a lifetime. I have come to know several social entrepreneurs in recent years, and most combine a business school brain with a social worker's heart. The triple convergence and the flattening of the world have been a godsend for them. Those who get it and are adapting to it have begun launching some very innovative projects.

Thomas L. Friedman
